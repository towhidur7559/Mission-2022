My Mission In 2022

Mission

Some people want to earn money, some want to earn fame and some want to serve others. I had to think a lot to choose my aim/profession/career in life. I think, it should be decided according to one's aptitude, ability and merit. Considering these, my choice is the simple wish to be a teacher.

Vision

Undoubtedly, teaching is a very noble profession. A teacher is like a potter who works on wheel to give shape to his pots, cutting out the rough edges to give a perfect finish to the end product. At present, neither the students nor the teachers have the same sincerity and singleness of purpose.

Goals

However, there are still some exceptional teachers who are genuinely concerned about the future of their pupils and try to guide them sincerely.

Developed with limits

One cannot overlook the advantages one has as a teacher. One gets so many holidays which can be used to catch up with other interests one neglects on working days. This is really a very tempting prospect. Besides, over the years the salary of teachers has also become very attractive.

Know my goal

I am willing to work hard to actualize my ambition, and waiting for the day when, after completing my training in teaching, I can become a teacher. That day indeed will be a red-letter day in my life.
